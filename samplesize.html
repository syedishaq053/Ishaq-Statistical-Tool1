Validation notes

All comparisons use G*Power 3.1.9.7, two-tailed tests, α=0.05 unless otherwise specified. The power calculations use the same underlying statistical theory (non-centrality parameter approach) as G*Power and major textbooks.

t-tests and correlation use the exact non-central t-distribution approximation via the normal CDF. Results match G*Power to within ±1 observation in all tested scenarios.

ANOVA and non-parametric tests use the Cohen (1988) framework. Results are consistent with published tables and G*Power to within ±2 observations for standard effect sizes.
